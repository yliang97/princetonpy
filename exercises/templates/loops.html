The primary format for publishing books and magazines—known as the CMYK format—specifies the level of cyan (C), magenta (M), yellow (Y), and black (K) on a real scale from 0.0 to 1.0.

    Write a program RGBtoCMYK.py that converts from RGB format to CMYK format. Your program should take three integer command-line arugments red, green, and blue; print the RGB values; then print the equivalent CMYK values using these mathematical formulas:
